About EmPACT Lab

Welcome to the Embodied Perception and Interaction Lab (EmPACT) Lab at NTU. We are dedicated to advancing the frontiers of AI and embodied intelligent systems through AI-driven perception and interaction approaches. Our research focuses on making intelligent agents that can sense, perseive, and interact with the world in more natural and efficient ways.

Our lab explores various cutting-edge topics including Neuro- and Physics-driven Sensing, Human-like Multi-Sensory Fusion, Spatial Intelligence, Embodied Learning Theory, Human-Agent Interaction. We aim to publish top-tier research in world-class conferences and journals while fostering the next generation of researchers in AI and robotics.

We are focusing on enabling human-centered intelligent agents to sense, perceive and cognize and interact with environement in a human-like manner!
